How it works

1Scan your plastic cards

The camera reads any barcode or QR in a second — card covers are cropped automatically.

2Show at checkout

A bright, scanner-ready code on screen. Works without internet.

3The right card, right there

Kodnyk recommends the cards you use near your current location — in the app and on the widget.

Scan anything

Barcode or QR — from the camera, a photo, or typed by hand. The card cover is auto-cropped like a document.

📷

Works offline

Cards live on your device and render on-screen with no network. The queue at the checkout won't wait.

✈️

Smart nearby

Kodnyk remembers where you use each card and recommends the right one when you're back in the store.

📍
🔒

Yours only

Synced securely, protected by per-user rules. Export everything as JSON or erase your account in one tap.

📲

Home-screen widget

Your cards right on the iPhone home screen — nearby ones first, favourites next. Flip through and scan without opening the app.

⭐

Favourites & your order

Star the cards you use most and drag the grid into your own order — just like home-screen icons.
